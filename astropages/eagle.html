Messier 16
The Eagle Nebula

Home of the Famous "Pillars of Creation"

The Eagle Nebula is an emission nebula and star cluster located approximately 5700 light-years away. It is a part of a larger HII emission region, and liees in the spectacular Sagittarius arm of the Milky Way. A summer classic, the region has become infamous due to Hubble's images of the "Pillars of Creation", the central region which is a stellar birthplace (featured in the center of my image).
